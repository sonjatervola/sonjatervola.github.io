Hello and Welcome!

I am a PhD researcher at the University of Cambridge, supervised by Prof. Jenny Gibson in the Play and Communication Lab. My research focuses on how Artificial Intelligence and Social Robotics can support neurodivergent education and inclusive learning for all learners. I am funded by the Finnish Science Academy's Eino Jutikkala Fund and the Oskar Huttunen Foundation.
