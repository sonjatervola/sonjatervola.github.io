Agriscale- Smart Farming Project for Ugandan Farmers

This project focuses on developing innovative smart farming solutions to support Ugandan farmers in improving their agricultural practices through technology and AI-driven insights.

Project Overview

Smart Farming Solutions for Uganda

2021 - Present | University of Cambridge

This innovative project aims to revolutionize farming practices in Uganda by implementing smart farming technologies and AI-driven agricultural solutions. The project focuses on providing local farmers with accessible, affordable, and effective tools to improve crop yields and sustainable farming practices.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Crop Monitoring
  • Weather Prediction Systems
  • Soil Health Analysis
  • Mobile App for Farmers
  • Local Language Support
  • Offline Functionality

Project Impact

Community Engagement

Ugandan Farming Communities

2021 - Present

Direct collaboration with local farming communities to understand their needs and develop tailored solutions that address real-world challenges in Ugandan agriculture.

Technology Implementation

Smart Farming Infrastructure

2022 - Present

Deployment of IoT sensors, mobile applications, and AI algorithms to provide farmers with real-time insights and recommendations for optimal farming practices.
